The Best Movies From The Golden Age of Hollywood

My absolute fave movies from the 50s

Some Like It Hot

This movie is laugh-out-loud funny and features some of Marilyn Monroe's best onstage looks. I love the comedy aspects of this and the fact Marilyn held her own incredibly well with two amazing comediens shows her range. If you're looking for a good comfort movie, this is the one!

Diabolique

French Alfred Hitchcock meets pseudo lesbian love story. This is one of the best psychological thrillers I've seen and I highly reccomend. If a mistress and wife murdering a man together isn't a strong enough plot for you than I don't know what is!

Singing in the Rain

Technicolor was created for a moment like this. The imagery and cinemitography of this film are absolutely insane This movie is the musical of all musicals and I strongly believe that even someone who doesn't like musicals would thouroughly enjoy this bright, fun, entertaining and colorful film.
